# Startup Resources for Founders  
A curated collection of tools, directories and finance resources to help you build and grow your startup. Explore the sections below and visit our site [startup‑resources.github.io](https://fusiondesigner.github.io/startup-resources.github.io/) for more.  

## Tool Directories and Curated Lists  
- [Startup Stash](https://startupstash.com/) – a large directory of tools and resources covering categories like analytics, marketing, design and development.  
- [Founder Institute Startup Resource Lists](https://fi.co/startup-resources) – region‑specific lists of accelerators, incubators, investors and more.  
- [Awesome Startup Resources](https://github.com/ahmadnassri/awesome-startup-resources) – a curated GitHub list featuring general resources, learning materials, books, podcasts and tool categories.  

## Collaboration & Project Management Tools  
- [Trello](https://trello.com/) – a board‑based tool for organizing tasks and workflows.  
- [Asana](https://asana.com/) – a project management platform for planning and tracking team work.  

## Customer Relationship Management (CRM)  
- [HubSpot CRM](https://www.hubspot.com/products/crm) – a free CRM to organize leads and interactions.  
- [Zoho Bigin](https://www.zoho.com/bigin/) – a streamlined CRM for small businesses and startups.  

## Marketing Tools  
- [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/) – an email marketing platform for newsletters and campaigns.  
- [Buffer](https://buffer.com/) – a social media scheduling tool to plan and publish posts.  

## Finance & Funding Resources  
- [Lighter Capital Founders' Hub](https://www.lightercapital.com/founders-hub) – a library of guides on raising capital and mastering cash‑flow management.  

## AI Tools & Large Language Models (LLMs)  

### General & Creative AI Tools  
- [ChatGPT (OpenAI)](https://chat.openai.com) – AI assistant for writing, research, brainstorming, coding, and more.  
- [Google Gemini](https://ai.google/gemini) – Google’s multimodal AI with text, image, and reasoning capabilities.  
- [Claude (Anthropic)](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) – AI assistant focused on thoughtful reasoning and safe outputs.  
- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai) – AI research engine for factual information and summaries.  
- [Notion AI](https://www.notion.so/product/ai) – built‑in AI for notes, documents, and task automation.  
- [Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com) – AI image generation for visuals, branding, and creative content.  
- [Synthesia](https://www.synthesia.io) – AI video creation tool (text‑to‑video).  
- [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.com) – AI voice and speech generation studio.  
- [AI Tools Directory](https://aitoolsdirectory.com/) – searchable catalog of AI apps, models, and integrations.  

### Coding & Developer AI  
- [Cursor (AI code editor)](https://cursor.com/) – AI‑assisted code editing powered by natural language.  
- [Replit](https://replit.com/) – browser‑based coding environment with AI assistance.  
- [Claude Code](https://www.anthropic.com/claude-code) – AI coding assistant from Anthropic.  
- [v0.app](https://v0.app/) – build full apps with AI‑assisted development (integrated with the Vercel ecosystem).  
- [Lovable](https://lovable.ai) – AI platform enabling text‑to‑app generation.  

## App Hosting & Deployment Providers  

### Static & Frontend‑Focused  
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com) – edge hosting built for modern frontend frameworks.  
- [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com) – Git‑based deployments for static sites and JAMstack apps.  
- [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com) – global edge platform with built‑in CDN and security.  

### Full‑Stack & Flexible Hosting  
- [Render](https://render.com) – host web services, databases, and full apps from one platform.  
- [Fly.io](https://fly.io) – edge‑distributed deployment for full apps with global performance.  
- [Railway](https://railway.app) – infrastructure platform with simple database and backend support.  
- [DigitalOcean App Platform](https://www.digitalocean.com/products/app-platform) – PaaS for apps with predictable pricing.  
- [AWS Amplify](https://aws.amazon.com/amplify) – full‑stack serverless platform integrated with AWS.  

### Free & Lightweight Options  
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – free static site hosting directly from your repo.  
- [Firebase Hosting](https://firebase.google.com/products/hosting) – fast static and dynamic hosting with integrated backend.
